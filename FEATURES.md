# GenZ Cinema App - Feature List

This document outlines the core and advanced features for the **GenZ Cinema** application, a modern movie booking and discovery platform.

## 1. User Authentication & Profiles
- **Multi-method Login:** Social logins (Google, Apple ID, Facebook) and traditional Email/Password.
- **Biometric Authentication:** FaceID or Fingerprint for quick access.
- **Personalized Profiles:** Custom avatars, viewing history, and "Favorite" movies/genres.
- **Loyalty Program:** Reward points for every booking, tiers (Silver, Gold, Platinum).

## 2. Movie Discovery (The "GenZ" Experience)
- **Dynamic Home Screen:** Trending movies, personalized recommendations based on AI, and "Coming Soon" trailers.
- **Advanced Search & Filter:** Search by title, actor, director, genre, and filter by rating (IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes) or language.
- **Short-form Content:** "Glimpse" - 15-30 second high-octane clips from trailers (TikTok/Reels style) to drive engagement.
- **Voice Search:** Find movies using natural language.

## 3. Booking & Ticketing
- **Interactive Seat Map:** High-fidelity, zoomable seat selection with real-time availability updates.
- **Combo Deals:** Pre-booking snacks and drinks (Popcorn, Soda, Nachos) with the ticket.
- **Flexible Payments:** Support for Credit/Debit cards, Digital Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and "Buy Now, Pay Later" options.
- **E-Tickets (QR Codes):** Paperless gating with tickets stored in-app and integrated with system wallets (Apple Wallet/Google Wallet).

## 4. Cinema & Showtimes
- **Location-Based Services:** Find the nearest cinemas using GPS.
- **Cinema Details:** Amenities (IMAX, 4DX, Recliner seats, Dolby Atmos), parking info, and directions.
- **Real-time Showtimes:** Dynamic scheduling with "Last Minute" deal alerts.

## 5. Social & Engagement
- **Watch Party:** Invite friends to book seats in the same row/section easily.
- **In-app Reviews & Ratings:** Share opinions and see what friends are watching.
- **Spoiler-free Mode:** Toggle to hide reviews or plot points for unreleased or recently released movies.
- **Movie Trivia & Quiz:** Interactive games to earn reward points while waiting for the show.

## 6. Notifications & Alerts
- **Reminders:** Push notifications for upcoming shows (1 hour before).
- **Waitlist:** Get notified when tickets for a blockbuster movie go on sale.
- **Personalized Offers:** Discount codes for preferred genres or birthday specials.

## 7. Admin & Management (Internal)
- **Movie Management:** Add/Edit/Delete movie listings, trailers, and posters.
- **Screening Controls:** Manage showtimes, theater assignments, and seat layouts.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Revenue tracking, occupancy rates, and user engagement metrics.
- **Promotion Manager:** Create and manage discount codes and loyalty campaigns.

## 8. Premium Experience (The "Pro" Features)
- **AR Seat Preview:** Augmented Reality view of the screen from the chosen seat before booking.
- **Gift Cards:** Purchase and send digital gift cards to friends.
- **Dark/Light Mode:** Seamless theme switching with high-quality gradients and blur effects (Glassmorphism).
- **Multi-language Support:** Localized UI for a global audience.
